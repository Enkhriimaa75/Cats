All Cat Breeds

Discover different types of cats from around the world

Persian Cat

From: Iran | Size: Medium to large

Weight: Males 9-14 lbs, Females 7-11 lbs

Coat: Long, thick, glossy

Lifespan: 12 to 17 years

Temperament

Calm, gentle, and sweet. Affectionate companions who enjoy peaceful environments. Prefer lounging to climbing. Not particularly vocal, communicate with soft voices.

Grooming

Extensive grooming required. Daily brushing essential. Eyes need daily wiping. Regular baths every few weeks.

Health Issues

  • Polycystic kidney disease
  • Breathing difficulties
  • Dental malocclusions
  • Eye conditions
  • Hypertrophic cardiomyopathy

Siamese Cat

From: Thailand | Size: Medium

Weight: Males 11-15 lbs, Females 8-12 lbs

Coat: Short, fine, glossy

Lifespan: 12 to 20 years

Temperament

Extremely intelligent, social, and vocal. Love to communicate through loud meows. Form deep bonds. Don't like being alone. Playful, energetic, and curious.

Grooming

Short coat requires minimal grooming. Weekly brushing sufficient. Generally good at self-grooming.

Health Issues

  • Dental disease
  • Respiratory issues
  • Progressive retinal atrophy
  • Amyloidosis

Maine Coon

From: United States | Size: Large

Weight: Males 12-15 lbs, Females 9-12 lbs

Coat: Long, shaggy, water-resistant

Lifespan: 12 to 15 years

Temperament

Incredibly friendly, sociable, good-natured. Gentle and patient despite large size. Excellent family pets. Playful well into adulthood. Intelligent, can be trained.

Grooming

Moderate grooming needed. Brushing two to three times per week prevents matting.

Health Issues

  • Hypertrophic cardiomyopathy
  • Hip dysplasia
  • Spinal muscular atrophy

Bengal Cat

From: United States | Size: Medium to large

Weight: Males 10-18 lbs, Females 6-12 lbs

Coat: Short, spotted/marbled with glittery sheen

Lifespan: 12 to 16 years

Temperament

Highly active, intelligent, playful. Curious explorers who love to climb and jump. Many enjoy playing with water. Require plenty of mental and physical stimulation.

Grooming

Short coat easy to maintain. Weekly brushing sufficient. Generally enjoy grooming sessions.

Health Issues

  • Progressive retinal atrophy
  • Hypertrophic cardiomyopathy
  • Patellar luxation

British Shorthair

From: United Kingdom | Size: Medium to large

Weight: Males 12-18 lbs, Females 9-15 lbs

Coat: Short, plush, dense

Lifespan: 12 to 17 years

Temperament

Calm, easygoing, affectionate without being demanding. Independent but appreciate companionship. Patient with children and pets. Perfect for quiet homes.

Grooming

Easy to maintain. Weekly brushing sufficient. More frequent brushing during shedding season.

Health Issues

  • Hypertrophic cardiomyopathy
  • Polycystic kidney disease
  • Obesity

Ragdoll Cat

From: United States | Size: Large

Weight: 10-15 lbs

Coat: Semi-long, plush, silky

Lifespan: 13 to 18 years

Temperament

Extremely gentle, calm, affectionate. Love being held. Go limp when picked up. Very social, follow owners room to room. Patient and tolerant. Not aggressive.

Grooming

Relatively easy despite long coat. Fur doesn't mat easily. Regular brushing twice a week keeps coat in good condition.

Health Issues

  • Hypertrophic cardiomyopathy
  • Polycystic kidney disease
  • Bladder stones

Abyssinian Cat

From: Ethiopia | Size: Small to medium

Weight: Males 7-10 lbs, Females 6-8 lbs

Coat: Short, fine, ticked

Lifespan: 9 to 15 years

Temperament

Extremely active, curious, playful. Love to explore, climb, investigate everything. Highly intelligent. Enjoy interactive play and puzzle toys. Form strong bonds with families.

Grooming

Short coat requires minimal grooming. Weekly brushing usually sufficient to keep coat healthy and shiny.

Health Issues

  • Progressive retinal atrophy
  • Pyruvate kinase deficiency
  • Renal amyloidosis

Scottish Fold

From: Scotland | Size: Medium

Weight: Males 9-13 lbs, Females 6-9 lbs

Coat: Short or long, dense, plush

Lifespan: 11 to 15 years

Temperament

Sweet-tempered, calm, affectionate. Adaptable, get along well with children and pets. Not overly vocal, have soft voices. Playful but not hyperactive.

Grooming

Shorthair needs weekly brushing. Longhair requires more frequent grooming to prevent matting. Ears need regular checking and cleaning.

Health Issues

  • Osteochondrodysplasia
  • Degenerative joint disease
  • Cardiomyopathy

Sphynx Cat

From: Canada | Size: Medium

Weight: Males 8-12 lbs, Females 6-9 lbs

Coat: Hairless (some have fine down)

Lifespan: 8 to 14 years

Temperament

Extremely friendly, energetic, affectionate. Outgoing, love being center of attention. Playful, mischievous nature. Perform silly antics. Highly social, don't like being alone.

Grooming

Regular bathing required (weekly or bi-weekly) because oils accumulate on skin. Ears need frequent cleaning. Must be kept warm in cold weather.

Health Issues

  • Hypertrophic cardiomyopathy
  • Hereditary myopathy
  • Skin conditions
  • Sensitivity to temperature extremes
